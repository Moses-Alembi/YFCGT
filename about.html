About Us

Youth for Christ Got Talent (YFCGT) is a project of the Ministry of Youth Affairs, Creative Economy and Sports under the Talanta Hela initiative,

We are dedicated to fostering interfaith dialogue and understanding among youth to passionately discover, cultivate, and amplify talents, transforming lives and communities through a commitment to excellence, inclusivity, and continuous personal and professional development thus a catalyst for a world where every talent is not only discovered but also cultivated into a force for positive change within the church set up and beyond for God and for the country.

Mission:


Empowering Young Kenyans to Shine: Through Faith, Talent, Community and church.

Vision:


To ignite a vibrant community where young Kenyans discover and share their God-given talents, fostering spiritual growth, self-confidence, and positive impact across the nation.

Core Values

Faithfulness: Rooted in Christian principles, we celebrate God's gifts and inspire young people to live their lives with purpose and integrity
Creativity: We embrace diverse talents and artistic expressions, providing a platform for young people to showcase their unique skills and passions.
Community: We build a supportive network of mentors, peers, and families, fostering collaboration, encouragement, and lifelong connections.
Excellence: We strive for high standards in performance and production, empowering young people to reach their full potential and inspire others.
Impact: We believe in the transformative power of talent, using it to address social issues, raise awareness, and contribute to positive change in Kenya.

Our Activities

- Organize regular talent shows across Kenya, featuring music, dance, spoken word, visual arts, pageantry and other creative expressions.


- Provide training workshops and mentorship programs to equip young people with performance skills, stage presence, and personal development tools.


- Partner with institutions, local churches, schools, and community organizations to reach a wider audience and create a sustainable impact.


- Organize outreach initiatives that leverage talent to address social issues like poverty, health awareness, and environmental protection.


- Foster a culture of giving back, encouraging young people to use their talents to serve their communities and make a positive difference in the world.
