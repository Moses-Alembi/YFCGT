Our Work Activities

Talent Show

Organize regular talent shows across Kenya, featuring music, dance, spoken word, visual arts, pageantry and other creative expressions.

Mentorship-Workshop Programmes

Provide training workshops and mentorship programs to equip young people with performance skills, stage presence, and personal development tools.

Partnerships

We Partner with institutions, local churches, schools, and community organizations to reach a wider audience and create a sustainable impact

Outreach Initiatives

We organize outreach initiatives that leverage talent to address social issues like poverty, health awareness, and environmental protection

YOUTH FOR CHRIST GOT TALENT

Youth for Christ Got Talent (YFCGT) is a project of the Ministry of Youth Affairs, Creative Economy and Sports under the Talanta Hela initiative,We are dedicated to fostering interfaith dialogue and understanding among youth to passionately discover, cultivate, and amplify talents, transforming lives and communities through a commitment to excellence, inclusivity, and continuous personal and professional development thus a catalyst for a world where every talent is not only discovered but also cultivated into a force for positive change within the church set up and beyond for God and for the country
